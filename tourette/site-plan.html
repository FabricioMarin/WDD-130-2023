Home page

People who are interested in knowing more about Tourette syndrome or who may have been diagnosed with this syndrome will be curious to know more about it, so on the Home page they will have an introduction to what Tourette syndrome is and its characteristics. Along with an introductory video of what it is. The video will explain what tourrete syndrome is, what is it the characteristics and will introduce some people with their experiences. this home page will have two more pages, "experiences", will have experiences about people will tourrete syndrome, and the other page will be "questions", this page will answer some questions that people will have about this particular syndrome. This home page will be an important introductory to make people want to know more about tourette syndrome and try to discover more about it. This home page will help people to understand the importance to learn or study any syndrome

Experiences

Not only by seeking to know what Tourette syndrome is, you will obtain testimonies and experiences about people who live with it every day of their lives, to help people understand how people with Tourette syndrome feel about this syndrome, in addition to helping to those who have this syndrome how to cope with their life with Tourette syndrome. I will focuse in some testimonies of Linda and her son Lucas. Linda studied a CDC-sponsored Tourette Association of America program on comprehensive behavioral intervention for tics. Other experiences will be about Mike, this is some of his introductory words, “My name is Mike Higgins and I am a father, pastor, husband, dean of students at a seminary, minister, colonel in the United States Army and I have Tourette syndrome.” Also I will share the experience of Tim Howard , The North Brunswick goalkeeper spent nine years at Everton, after traveling from the United States to England with Manchester United in 2003. He was the goalkeeper of the national team of the American country, but what many people do not know is that he suffered from a disease called Tourette syndrome since childhood. For that reason, his is a story of improvement within football.
